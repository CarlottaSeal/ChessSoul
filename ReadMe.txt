Carlotta's Starship (First Playable Version)!
To Beyond and Infinity!

Rules:
Press 'ESC' to enter the attract mode;
Press 'ESC' to quit the game when being in the attract mode;
Press ' ' or 'N' to quit the attract mode;
Press 'E' to accelerate;
Press 'S' to turn counterclockwise;
Press 'F' to turn clockwise;
Press ' ' to launch bullets;
Press 'N' to launch a new starship;

with Xbox controller:
Use Left Joystick to move & dash & turn around;
Press START to launch a new starship;

The game has 5 waves of Enemies;
The starship has 3 opportunities to rebirth, if the extra life is down to 0, the game will go back to the attract mode;

If the bullet hits the Enemy, the Enemy will lose a point of health;
the total health of every asteroid is 3;
the total health of every wasp is 2;
the total health of every beetle is 2;

If the starship collides the Enemy, the starship will die;
the asteroid will lose a point of health;
the wasp will lose a point of health;
the beetle will lose a point of health;


BE CAREFULL!! 
The max number of bullets simultaneously existing on the screen will be 20, or will open a debug window;
The max number of asteroids simultaneously existing on the screen will be 12, or will open a debug window;

Press 'F1' to Debug;
Press 'F8' to restart the game;
